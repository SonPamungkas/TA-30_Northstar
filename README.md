## Description

The definitive culmination of "Project: Northstar," originally engineered to train Cosmonauts across Primeva in advanced spacecraft operations. This overhaul transforms a once-docile, subsonic introductory airframe into a hard-hitting, supersonic strike platform. Every core system has been completely redesigned—including propulsion, avionics, power distribution, structural integrity, and flight control software.

## Key Features

### ⚡ Next-Gen Propulsion

* **Enhanced Baseline:** Driven by propulsion tech originally engineered to simulate high-G planetary departures, engine thrust is boosted to **60,000 N per engine** (~2.1× stock). The Northstar accelerates decisively through the transonic regime and sustains low-altitude supersonic cruise without sacrificing fuel economy.
* **Scramjet Mode:** Utilizing an atmospheric-intake bypass system originally meant for extraterrestrial environment, the engines automatically transition into scramjet operation above **Mach 0.9**, ramping output to **120,000 N per engine**. Built-in hysteresis prevents power flicker near the threshold—a legacy fix from early, turbulent test flights over the Primeva wastes. A pulsing **SCRAMJET ACTIVE** indicator flashes on the HUD when live.
* **Flight Ceiling:** Features a flat thrust curve up to **22,000 m**, maintaining peak power output right up to the absolute atmospheric flameout limit, allowing pilots to touch the very edge of the void before dropping back down for a strike run.

### 📡 Advanced Avionics & Power

* **EOTS (Electro-Optical Targeting):** Visual tracking range extended to **25 km** with 4× magnification. Originally calibrated to spot high-velocity orbital debris during cosmonaut training, the suite has been weaponized to lock onto combat targets moving up to 3,000 m/s.
* **Laser Designator:** Effective range boosted to **25 km**. The baseline civilian telemetry array has been replaced with a military-grade multi-spectral grid originally meant for HALE assets, expanding simultaneous tracking capabilities from 2 to **50 targets** for sweeping battlefield suppression.
* **Power Grid:** By retrofitting the airframe with heavy-duty solid-state capacitors meant for deep-space probes, system capacity, recharge rates, and max output received a massive **5× increase** to ensure high-draw weapon systems stay online during prolonged combat.

### 🛡️ Airframe Hardening & Control

* **Structural Integrity:** To survive the punishing kinetic and thermal stresses of low-altitude supersonic flight, part durability is increased **3×** to absorb severe combat damage. Airbrake joints are heavily reinforced with titanium-carbon weaves for safe, high-speed deployment.
* **Fly-By-Wire (FBW) Tuning:** The restrictive training-commission safety limiters have been completely overwritten. Custom automatic pitch and roll damping now activates above **Mach 1.0** to eliminate the violent transonic aerodynamic instability that plagued early prototypes.
* **Thermal/Speed Tolerances:** Cockpit overspeed warnings safely suppressed up to **800 m/s**, unlocking the true, unrestricted physical threshold of Project: Northstar's final iteration.
